About This Tool

Our Online Notepad is a simple, fast, and efficient way to take notes directly in your browser. Whether you need to jot down quick ideas, create a to-do list, or draft content, this distraction-free editor makes it easy. Your notes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage, so you won't lose your work if you accidentally close the tab.

Features

  • check_circle Distraction-free writing environment
  • check_circle Auto-save functionality
  • check_circle Export notes as TXT, PDF, or HTML
  • check_circle Word and character count

How to Use

  1. Simply start typing in the text area to create your note
  2. Your note is automatically saved as you type
  3. Use the export options to download your note in your preferred format
