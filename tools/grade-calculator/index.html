About Grading Scales

Grading scales can vary between institutions. The scales shown here are common examples, but your school or university may use a different scale.

Some institutions may also use different GPA values for the same letter grades, or may have additional grades like "Pass/Fail" or "Incomplete" that are not shown here.

Always check your institution's specific grading policy for the most accurate information.
